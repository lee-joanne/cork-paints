Who are we?


We are a group of local volunteer artists in Cork, Ireland coming together and ensuring that art is still alive in the city. We love to paint and have been painting for many years and want to teach those who are interested in painting at a low and affordable cost. By day we are your baristas, customer care support agent, or librarian. By night, we help you learn our skills in painting.
